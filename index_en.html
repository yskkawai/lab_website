Research

Research

🧬

Human Genomic Diversity

We analyze large-scale genome sequencing data to characterize genomic diversity in East Asian populations, including the Japanese population.

🌏

Human Evolution & Population History

Using genomic information, we investigate the history of human migration and dispersal, and the relationships between populations through statistical genetics.

🏥

Disease Genomics

We apply population genomics approaches to disease research, contributing to the identification of disease susceptibility variants and the advancement of genomic medicine.

💻

Bioinformatics

We develop and maintain genome databases, genotyping arrays, and imputation servers to support population-scale genomic analyses.
